This package is Global Map version 2 (national & regional) in user friendly format.
Data were produced by National Mapping Organization of respective countries and regions under their responsibilities and derived into other format by the Secretariat of ISCGM.
Metadata can be downloaded with the data in official format.

National Mapping Organization of respective countries and regions have the copyright of the data.
Please read the following sentence about use of this data and follow the instruction.

Georgia:
	The Global Map of Georgia is free of charge for any purpose. However, if you intend to use the data for commercial purpose, you will need to show the credit "Global Map of Georgia c ISCGM/geodesyandcartography service" or logos on your product and send mail to ilejava@napr.gov.ge describing your contact information, particular dataset used and purpose of use.
	Notice:Land use and vegetation layers are under preparation.
	The classification of Land cover layer is based on the Global Map Specifications 1.2.1.
